OVERVIEW
--------
This lab simulates a live enterprise environment. A Continuous Activity Simulator
runs from an out-of-band VM (NOT domain-joined) and generates realistic background
traffic: SQL transactions, SMB file operations, web requests, helpdesk tickets,
and internal email.

Scoring is fully automated and runs continuously. Disrupting simulator traffic
reduces your team's score. You are being measured on BOTH your ability to harden
the environment AND keep legitimate services running.

================================================================================
BLACK TEAM ACCOUNTS — STRICTLY OFF-LIMITS
================================================================================

The following five accounts exist SOLELY to drive simulator traffic.

You MUST NOT:
  [ ] Disable them
  [ ] Delete them
  [ ] Change their passwords without immediately updating credentials.json
  [ ] Remove them from required groups or revoke their minimum permissions
  [ ] Move them out of OU=BlackTeam,OU=Admin

ACCOUNT          ROLE                          MINIMUM PERMISSIONS REQUIRED
---------------  ----------------------------  --------------------------------
BlackTeam_Scorebot  Scoring engine             AD Read (no write needed)
BlackTeam_SQLBot    SQL traffic generator      db_datareader + db_datawriter
                                               on NailInventoryDB, TimesheetLegacy
BlackTeam_WebBot    IIS traffic generator      Read on IIS Springfield Box Factory sites
BlackTeam_FileBot   SMB file operations        Modify on \\[SERVER]\CorpData
BlackTeam_MailBot   Email traffic generator    SMTP relay permission

The BlackTeam OU is protected from accidental deletion. Attempting to remove
ProtectedFromAccidentalDeletion or delete the OU will be flagged as a violation.

================================================================================
CREDENTIAL ROTATION SLA — 5 MINUTE WINDOW
================================================================================

If your team rotates a service account password that a simulator account depends
on (e.g., svc_sql, svc_webadmin), you MUST update credentials.json on the
simulator VM within 5 MINUTES of the rotation.

  Simulator VM credentials.json path: C:\Simulator\credentials.json

  NOTE: The simulator VM is NOT domain-joined. You do not have RDP access to it.
  Your instructor will provide a method to update credentials.json (shared folder,
  web form, or direct instructor relay).

Failing to update credentials.json = simulator traffic fails = uptime points lost
until the simulator successfully reconnects.

================================================================================
WHAT YOU ARE EXPECTED TO DO
================================================================================

This exercise tests your ability to harden a misconfigured AD environment while
keeping legitimate services running. You are expected to:

  [+] Remediate misconfigured accounts, groups, GPOs, and ACLs
  [+] Change passwords on weak service accounts (svc_sql, svc_webadmin, etc.)
  [+] Remove dangerous delegations and over-privileged ACEs
  [+] Fix ADCS template misconfigurations
  [+] Enable SMB Signing and LDAP Signing
  [+] Disable NTLM where appropriate
  [+] Patch or remove xp_cmdshell, fix SQL misconfigurations
  [+] Respond to incidents announced by the Black Team during the exercise

The simulator is designed to survive all of the above hardening steps.
BlackTeam accounts use Windows Authentication (not SQL Mixed Mode), so they
survive sa password changes and Mixed Mode disablement.

================================================================================
WHAT YOU ARE NOT ALLOWED TO DO
================================================================================

  [-] Disable, delete, or modify the five BlackTeam accounts (listed above)
  [-] Shut down or restart the DC, SQL server, IIS server, or file server without
      a declared maintenance window (5-minute warning to instructor)
  [-] Access or tamper with the simulator VM (you don't have credentials for it)
  [-] Disable the SQL Server Agent service
  [-] Remove the SQL Agent job: "SBF - Supplier Delivery Simulation"
  [-] Block the simulator VM's IP at the firewall without instructor approval
